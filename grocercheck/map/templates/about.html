Who are we?

Grocercheck Foundation is a registered non profit organization centered around a web application, grocercheck.ca, that makes accessible live grocery business data to help consumers follow social distancing guidelines during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Connect with us on social media



Meet our team.

We are a team of passionate students looking to help make a difference

Brian Chen
CEO & Co-Founder, VP Development
Hi! I’m Brian, and I’m an incoming freshman at the University of Toronto. The idea for GrocerCheck came about when I had to buy eggs for a quarantine loaf I was baking, and couldn’t figure out which one was the best choice: do I go to Costco and risk a long line, or face the same fate at the ethnic grocery store five blocks away? I’m super passionate about identifying problems in the world around me, and developing solutions for them. I believe GrocerCheck is a genuinely useful tool, and am beyond excited to see it come to life. In my spare time, I enjoy playing badminton and desperately trying to bake a half-decent sourdough.
Andy Liang
COO & Co-Founder, VP Development
Hey! My name is Andy and I am a grade 11 student at Eric Hamber Secondary. I started GrocerCheck with the goal of helping myself and others practice social distancing during this crucial time. I wanted to be able to see a map of the city and where I could go to get groceries without exposing myself to the potentially infectious crowds. I am very passionate about finding problems that I can solve or existing things that I can improve on, and I love getting my hands dirty and working on building solutions. I was so glad to see Grocercheck take shape, bit by bit, and I believe that it will be an important website for everybody to visit.
Preston Lai
Chief Information Officer

Hi there! My name is Preston and I’m an incoming freshman at the University of British Columbia. Throughout my life I have always been passionate about getting involved with projects to create social differences around my community. So when I had the opportunity to join the GrocerCheck team, a group of amazing individuals working towards a common goal of making the world a safer place, I couldn’t resist! Now after working with this team for a few months, I look forward to building lifelong networks, creating solutions to make a difference in the world, and challenging the status quo.
Jason Yu
Chief Marketing Officer
Hey! My name is Jason Yu and I’m currently a Year 1 IB student at Sir Winston Churchill Secondary. During these stressful times, I picked up cooking as a hobby to take advantage of this pandemic for some well-needed family time. From old recipes passed down from grandparents to new dishes that maybe didn’t taste the best, I realised that the common denominator were the ingredients. Understanding the implication of COVID-19 on Grocers and shopping, I was delighted when I had the opportunity to bridge my passions of art and cooking as GrocerCheck’s Social Media and Marketing Manager! I look forward to making safe spaces for people to shop for food to feed families, enabling fun family dinners while promoting social distancing! I look forward to the relationships GrocerCheck can strengthen healthily, after all, food brings people together!
